My Story


I am originally from North Carolina and currently reside in Seattle. I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Biology from East Carolina University. I was on the path to medical school before discovering my interest in technology.

In 2017, I completed a coding bootcamp with a core focus on frontend development before beginning my career as a Software Engineer at Red Hat.

I am currently a Senior Product Manager at Meta working in the VR space. Previously, I worked as a PM at Microsoft where I focused on building tools for 6+ million VS & .NET developers.

I strive to empower others through technology and build products that add value to people's lives.
